The Context Camera (CTX) is an instrument on the Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter (MRO).

This is a composite image of 39 regional semi-controlled CTX mosaics at 5m/pixel for the 2015 proposed Mars Human Exploration zones. 

See https://astrogeology.usgs.gov/search/map/Mars/MarsReconnaissanceOrbiter/CTX/HumanExplorationZones/CTX_Human_EZ_sites_Oct2015
