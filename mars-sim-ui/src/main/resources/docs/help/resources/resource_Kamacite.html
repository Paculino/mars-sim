Resource : "Kamacite" (Solid)


1. Description :

  • An alloy of iron and nickel, may originate from meteorites. The proportion iron:nickel is between 90:10 and 95:5; small quantities of other elements, such as cobalt or carbon may also be present. The mineral has a metallic luster, is gray and has no clear cleavage although its crystal structure is isometric-hexoctahedral.
