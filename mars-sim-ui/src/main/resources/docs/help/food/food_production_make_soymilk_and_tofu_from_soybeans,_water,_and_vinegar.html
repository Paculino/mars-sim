Food Production "Make Soymilk And Tofu From Soybeans, Water, And Vinegar"

1. Description :

  • Boil up Soybeans, grind into soymilk, filter off okra, and add vinegar as coagulants. gently stir and heat, the curds will separate. Save and sweeten some soymilk.
