Food Production "Make Tofu from Soybeans, Water, and Epsom Salt (large batch)"

1. Description :

  • Boil up Soybeans, grind into soymilk, filter off okra, and add Epsom Salt as Coagulants. gently stir and heat, the curds will separate.
